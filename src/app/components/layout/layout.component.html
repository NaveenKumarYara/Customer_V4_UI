With Video Sizzle, hiring leaders are the directors of the film and the job is the star of the show. NEXTGEN Interview Platform

Robots conduct interviews for each uploaded profile. Third party interviewing experts with historical performance analytics are listed for your outsourcing needs.

The NEXTGEN Interview Platform immediately gets the hiring ball rolling. A list of third party interview geeks/gurus who are subject matter experts are chosen by hiring leaders saving a huge amount of time and increasing quality. You guessed it...it’s uber-style interviews! It includes a rating system of the geeks/gurus, their areas of expertise and fees, and an historical database of their previous interviews allowing hiring leaders to choose the highest rated geeks to participate in the selection process. A complete log is kept of all interviews for hiring leaders to review and provide ratings of the geeks/gurus who are part of the hiring leader’s hiring process. The rating system inherently raises the quality of geeks as their compensation is directly correlated to their rating.

Not a Dashboard...a Job Wall

Not a dashboard but a job wall providing analytics for the post.

A job wall is a wall of useful information representing the entire life of the posting. It includes the number of views, suggested profiles, likes and dislikes, hires, withdrawals, and rejections. Each candidate has a display revealing a decision summary, skill rating, personality fit, technical screening, and background and reference verification information. Without spending much time, hiring leaders can quickly scan through this condensed version of the candidate. It is a one-stop solution for the complete recruiting cycle.

AI Hiring Predictor

The AI Hiring Predictor provides data-driven forecasts for the hiring process.

The Hiring Predictor gives hiring leaders the realistic likelihood of hiring and the average timeline for the hiring to unfold.
